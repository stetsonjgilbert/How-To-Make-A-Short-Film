The first you need to make a movie is people willing to work with you. In film, its "teamwork makes the dreamwork." It is impossible to film a movie by yourself because you can't act film and control sound all at the same time. I would at least recommend two people for a slient film. However, the ideal least amount of people one should have in a film group is three (one actor (two actors if a slient film), a camera-man, and a sound recordist). The more, the better. Make sure you can settle on an idea before you start to plan your film.
