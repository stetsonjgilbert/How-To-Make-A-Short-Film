Post-production is arguably the hardest part of the entire movie-making process. You need to edit the entire film and make it look cohesive and style music it almost perfectly. This is the part that defines your movie from a video with green screens and grabbing at the air to a cinematic masterpiece. However, it take an insanely long amount of time to edit. For example, "The Odyssey" took 91 days to film but it took more than a year to edit. Luckily, there is a app that can help you:

Premeire Pro

As a person who has had experience with premeire pro, I recommned it because it is easy accessible and it is free. Also, it allows you to: cut your clips, download files, fix music and sound effects to your clips, add title and text effects, speed up and slow down your clips, zoom in on a speciifc part, and allows you to photoshop. This is definitely will make you feel like a top-tier editor from Hollywood. I wood definitely recommend this for your movie editing proccess.
